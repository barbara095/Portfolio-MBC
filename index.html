About Me

I work in market research with an academic background in Marketing and Business Management. People often describe me as curious, ambitious and patient, which is why I've taken an interest in learning the wonderfully complex language of coding.

I like to travel to both warm and cold places. My favourite destinations are Greece, which is my background, Aspen Colorado, New York and the Fijian islands. In my spare time I enjoy playing leisure sports such as Tennis as well as exercising including strength training, running and bike riding. y guilty pleasures include ice-cream, pizza and cheesecake of any varietal.

I am really enjoying the challenge of coding, where the possibilities are endless, and take great enjoyment in continually learning new methods and functions. I hope to one day work as a professional in the field of Web development, utilising both my creative and mathematical skills learnt throughout this coding course, and to work up towards becoming a full stack developer with more experience and practice.
